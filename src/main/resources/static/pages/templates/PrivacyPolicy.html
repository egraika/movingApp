(2) Information We Collect Through Automatic Data Collection Technologies.

As you navigate through and interact with the Move Muscle Platforms, we may use automatic data collection technologies to collect certain information about your browsing actions and patterns, including:

  • Details of your visits to the Move Muscle Platforms, including traffic data, location data, logs and other communication data and the resources that you access and use through the Move Muscle Platforms.
  • Information about your computer and internet connection, including your IP address, operating system and browser type.
  • We also may use these technologies to collect information about your online activities over time and across third-party websites or other online services (behavioral tracking).

The technologies we use for this automatic data collection may include:

  • Cookies (or browser cookies). A “cookie” is a small file placed on the hard drive of your computer by a website. We may use both session cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them) to provide you with a more personal and interactive experience on the Move Muscle Platforms. You may refuse to accept browser cookies by activating the “do not track” feature on your browser that lets you indicate that you do not want your online activities tracked. These features are not yet uniform, so we may not respond to such features or signals. At times, if you remove or reject cookies, it could affect how the Move Muscle Platforms work for you. Therefore, if you turn on a “do not track” feature, we and our third-party providers may continue collecting information about your online activities. However, if you select this setting you may be unable to access certain parts of the Move Muscle Platforms. Unless you have adjusted your browser setting so that it will refuse cookies, the Move Muscle Platforms will issue cookies when you direct your browser to the Move Muscle Platforms.
  • Flash Cookies. Certain features of the Move Muscle Platforms may use local stored objects (or flash cookies) to collect and store information about your preferences and navigation to, from and on the Move Muscle Platforms. Flash cookies are not managed by the same browser settings as are used for browser cookies. To learn how you can manage your Flash cookie settings, visit the Flash player settings page on Adobe’s website.
  • Web Beacons. Pages of the Move Muscle Platforms and our e-mails may contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also referred to as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs) that permit Move Muscle to, for example, count users who have visited those pages or opened an e-mail and for other related Service statistics (for example, recording the popularity of certain website content and verifying system and server integrity).
