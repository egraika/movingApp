Some of your questions

Are you licensed and insured?

The Moving Company is licensed and insured by the California Public Utilities Commission (CPUC). License number: Cal P.U.C. T-190610. To be insured by the CPUC, moving companies must prove their financial and safety fitness. They must also prove to the CPUC that they have adequate insurance, and they are subject to criminal background clearance by the California Department of Justice and Federal Bureau of Investigation.

If I`m planning on moving, how far in advance should I call Moving Company?

The sooner, the better. We accommodate all our customers’ schedules as best we can. To ensure you get the date(s) you want, call as soon as you know when you’ll be moving.

When are you available?

We are happy to serve you Monday-Saturday. The office hours are 9AM to 6PM and 9AM to 1PM on Saturday.

Will you move pianos?

Yes! We move most upright pianos, but not baby grand or grand pianos. Talk with your Moving Company representative ahead of time so we can make arrangements.

Will you conect/disconnect my appliences?

Yes! We ask you to sign a release of liability prior to hooking them up.

Do you move unique or very large items?

Yes! We move almost all items. Be sure to let your Movers representative know ahead of time in case it requires a third mover or special equipment.

Will you load/unload my truck, pods, etc.?

Yes! We will gladly load or unload you truck or pods.

Are there any hidden charges?

NO! We are very forward and upfront about our prices. We do NOT have any hidden charges or fees, just the hourly rate and the travel charge that never changes.
