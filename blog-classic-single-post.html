Glasgow chamber of commerce

Moving Company is proud to announce that we have been selected as a finalist in the category Excellence in Customer Service by the Glasgow Regional Chamber of Commerce Being listed as one the of the top 6 companies out of a field of over 245,000 Southern California Businesses is a great achievement.

Companies were judged on the following criteria: Educated sales and services representatives who are approachable, available and willing to help. High-quality service from beginning to end; the amount and quality of assistance given to customers. Responsiveness and follow-through once a problem has been identified; dealing with situations of adversity from customers. The Moving Company appreciates the recognition and will continue to maintain our commitment to excellence in customer service. As always, thank you for your continued support.

Moving Company offers premier moving services to make your business move simple. Our movers pay great attention to the care of your items and strive to provide you with a quick and effortless commercial movers move. Our movers are experienced and trained to effectively handle, transport, and install your furniture and office equipment. Since service is our primary focus, we ensure that Moving Company will exceed your satisfaction as your business mover. We will work with your budget to create a cost-efficient solution to move your business. From a small office to a large corporation, whether you are moving locally within Glasgow or long distance, Moving Company is the moving company you can trust.

We charge a simple hourly rate plus a one-time travel charge. In order to help determine your moving needs, we can quickly and easily provide a phone estimate. Based on the amount of furniture, boxes, and other items you have, we will give you an individualized quote. We are also more than happy to provide an on-site visual estimate for larger or more complex local moves. Packing services are also available - whether you need the whole house packed, or just one room, we are pleased to take care of it for you. We take great pride in all of our moves.
